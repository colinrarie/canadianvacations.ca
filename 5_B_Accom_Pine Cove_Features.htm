Pine Cove Cabin is situated on beautiful Dogtooth Lake, and is just a short boat ride (½ mile) from the Canadian Vacations RV Park.  Recently renovated, this 1500 sq. ft., fully modern, three bedroom cabin is quality lodging for up to 7 people.  This cabin also features a large kitchen and dining area, 1 ½ bathrooms, living room, three season sunroom and other luxuries, such as:

  • New water system with 3 filters and UV purification to guarantee bottled water quality at all taps

  • Air conditioning/ baseboard electric heat

  • New large TV and a VCR/DVD player

  • 110 channel satellite TV dish reception

  • Dishwasher, microwave and chest freezer

  • New large refrigerator and electric range

  • Ample glassware, silverware, dishes and cooking utensils

  • Extra refrigerator for beverages and bait

  • All new linens, bedding and towels

  • All new beds, single and king-size

  • Spare pillows, blankets and beach towels

  • Large propane BBQ

  • Outdoor fireplace and lawn chairs

  • Free use of canoe and paddles

  • Large dock with electric service, night light and a dock ladder for swimming

  • Free mainland parking, boat launch and docking
