Located in a private setting on beautiful Dogtooth Lake, Pine Point Cabin is accessed by water, ½ mile from the campground at Canadian Vacations.  It has been recently remodeled to provide a spacious 1300 sq. ft., family friendly, three bedroom deluxe accommodation for up to 8 people.  This cabin also features a large kitchen and dining area, 3 piece bathroom, living room, large screened porch for outdoor eating and separate screened room for barbequing.

Included:

  • New water system with 3 filters and UV purification to guarantee bottled water quality at all taps

  • Air conditioning / Baseboard electric heat

  • New large TV and a VCR/DVD player

  • 110 channel satellite TV

  • Dishwasher, microwave and chest freezer

  • Large refrigerator and electric range

  • Ample glassware, silverware, dishes and cooking utensils

  • Extra refrigerator for beer, pop and bait located in the porch

  • All newer linens, bedding and towels

  • Spare pillows, blankets and beach towels

  • Propane BBQ in a bug-free screened-in area

  • Docking for 4 boats, with electric service, dock ladder for swimming

  • Outdoor fire-pit and lawn chairs

  • Free use of Canoe and paddles

  • Free mainland parking, boat launching and docking
